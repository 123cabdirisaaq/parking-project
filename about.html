Parking Slot Management System

Parking Slot Management System waa nidaam casri ah oo loogu talagalay in lagu maareeyo goobaha baarkinka si fudud oo hufan. Nidaamkan wuxuu ka caawinayaa darawallada iyo maamulayaasha baarkinka in ay si sahlan u ogaadaan meelaha bannaan, meelaha buuxsamay, iyo xogta baarkinka waqtiga dhabta ah. Ujeedada nidaamkan waa in la yareeyo waqtiga lagu lumiyo raadinta meel bannaan, la hagaajiyo nidaamka baarkinka, isla markaana la kordhiyo waxtarka iyo amniga goobaha baarkinka.
